# Monu Settings - User Guide

## Accessing Settings

There are two ways to access the Settings screen:

### Method 1: From Profile Screen
1. Tap the **Profile** tab (bottom right, last tab)
2. Tap the **gear icon** (⚙️) in the top-right corner
3. Select **"⚙️ Cài đặt"** (Settings) from the dropdown menu

### Method 2: Quick Access
Settings are directly accessible from the Profile dropdown as the first option.

## Theme Settings

### Changing Your Theme

Monu offers three carefully designed themes to suit your preferences:

#### 1. **Dark Theme (🌙)** - Default
- **Best for:** Night listening, reduced eye strain, Monu's signature purple aesthetic
- **Colors:** Deep purples, dark backgrounds with vibrant accents
- **Perfect for:** Late-night sessions, comfortable evening listening
- **How to use:**
  - Tap the Dark theme option (first card)
  - Checkmark appears on active theme
  - Changes apply instantly

#### 2. **Light Theme (☀️)**
- **Best for:** Daytime use, bright environments, clean aesthetic
- **Colors:** Bright whites, soft neutrals with accent colors
- **Perfect for:** Office work, reading playlist descriptions
- **How to use:**
  - Tap the Light theme option (second card)
  - Entire app transforms to light mode
  - All text remains readable and accessible

#### 3. **Classic Theme (✨)**
- **Best for:** AMOLED displays, premium feel, golden accents
- **Colors:** Pure blacks, golden/orange highlights, luxury aesthetic
- **Perfect for:** Users who want a distinctive, premium experience
- **How to use:**
  - Tap the Classic theme option (third card)
  - Unique color palette applies throughout app

### Following Your System Settings

If you want Monu to automatically match your device's appearance:

1. In Settings, locate **"Follow System Settings"** toggle
2. Tap the toggle to enable (it turns blue/purple)
3. Monu will now:
   - Switch to Dark theme when device is in Dark mode
   - Switch to Light theme when device is in Light mode
   - Update automatically when you change device appearance

**Status indicator:** Shows current device appearance state

## Language Settings

### Switching Languages

Monu supports Vietnamese and English:

#### Vietnamese (🇻🇳)
- **Default language** for the app
- Complete interface translation
- All buttons, menus, and messages in Vietnamese

#### English (🇺🇸)
- Full English translation
- Useful for international users or testing
- All features available in English

### How to Change Language

1. In Settings, find the **"Language"** section
2. Tap your preferred language:
   - Tap **"Tiếng Việt (vi)"** for Vietnamese
   - Tap **"English (en)"** for English
3. Checkmark appears on selected language
4. App interface updates immediately

**Note:** Language preference is saved automatically. When you restart the app, your chosen language is restored.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will changing theme affect my music library?**
A: No. Your playlists, liked songs, and all data remain exactly the same. Theme only changes the visual appearance.

**Q: Can I switch between themes frequently?**
A: Yes! You can change themes as often as you like. Changes apply instantly with no data loss.

**Q: What if I enable "Follow System Settings" and then manually choose a theme?**
A: The manual theme selection takes priority. Your chosen theme will stay active until you enable "Follow System Settings" again.

**Q: Do I need internet to change settings?**
A: No. Theme and language settings are stored locally on your device.

**Q: Will my settings be preserved if I uninstall the app?**
A: No. Uninstalling removes all app data, including settings. You'll need to reconfigure after reinstalling.

**Q: Can I have different themes on different devices?**
A: Currently, settings are stored per device. Each device maintains its own preferences.

## Troubleshooting

### Theme not updating?
1. Make sure you tapped the theme option (you should see a checkmark)
2. Scroll through the app to see the changes
3. Close and reopen the app if changes don't appear

### Language not changing?
1. Verify you selected a different language (checkmark should appear)
2. Check if all text on screen has updated
3. Wait a moment for the update to fully process

### Settings not saving?
1. Close the app and reopen it
2. Check if your settings appear as you left them
3. If not, try again - there may be a temporary storage issue
